# Gaza Community Feedback and Complaint Mechanism Form

## Overview

This project is a KoboToolbox feedback and complaint mechanism form prepared for community-facing reporting and case intake workflows in Gaza. The form is designed to document complaints, feedback channels, case type, severity, and potential safeguarding or protection concerns in a clear bilingual structure.

## Project Goal

The form is intended to support organizations and field teams in receiving, documenting, and classifying community feedback and complaints in a standardized KoboToolbox workflow.

## Form Highlights

- Community feedback and complaint intake workflow
- Bilingual Arabic and English labeling
- Staff and submission channel documentation
- Complaint category and case classification
- Severity level assessment
- Safeguarding and protection risk flagging
- Structured free-text description section
- Suitable for accountability and case management support processes
