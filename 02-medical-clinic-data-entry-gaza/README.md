# Medical Clinic Data Entry Form - Gaza

## Overview

This project is a KoboToolbox medical data entry form designed for clinic-based and field-supported health registration workflows in Gaza. The form captures patient visit details, symptoms, referral source, and clinical measurements in a structured and deployment-ready XLSForm format.

## Project Goal

The form is intended to support medical teams, clinics, and enumerators with standardized health data entry for patient intake and follow-up documentation.

## Form Highlights

- Medical clinic intake and visit documentation
- Clinic and enumerator information section
- Patient symptom capture
- Referral source tracking
- Vital signs and measurements section
- Clear Kobo-friendly layout for operational use
- Suitable for health facility, mobile team, or phone follow-up workflows
